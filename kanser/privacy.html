Privacy Policy for Kanser AI

Effective Date: 12.08.2025

Kanser AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, Kanser AI.

1. Information We Collect

a. Account Information

We offer optional sign-in via Google or Apple to save your scanned skin scan history and preferences. When you choose to sign in, we collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Unique user identifier (provided by Google or Apple)

We do not access your passwords or any sensitive authentication data.

b. Scanned skin Data

When you scan a skin using our app, the following data may be collected:

  • Images of skin captured through the app
  • AI-generated analysis or metadata related to the scanned skin

This data is stored securely to allow you to revisit your scan history and access AI analysis results.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide AI-powered skin analysis
  • Save your scanned skin and associated data
  • Improve the performance and reliability of the app
  • Enable account-related features (like cloud storage of scans)

We do not use your data for advertising or third-party marketing.

3. Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All data is encrypted in transit and stored securely. User scan data is associated only with their account and is not shared with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Kanser integrates with OpenAI's API to provide AI skin scan details. When you use the app's skin detection feature, your skin image is sent to OpenAI's servers for process. OpenAI may have their own data collection and usage policies, which are governed by their privacy policy.

We integrate with the following third-party services solely for login functionality:

  • Google Sign-In
  • Apple Sign-In

These services provide us with limited personal information strictly for authentication purposes.

5. Your Privacy Choices

You can:

  • Use the app without signing in (with limited functionality)
  • Delete your account and all associated scan data upon request inside app
  • Revoke app access from your Google or Apple account settings

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes via the app or by updating the "Effective Date" above.
