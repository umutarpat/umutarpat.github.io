Privacy Policy for Altinos

Effective Date: 10.06.2025

Altinos ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, Altinos.

1. Information We Collect

a. Account Information

We offer optional sign-in via Google or Apple to save your scanned gold history and preferences. When you choose to sign in, we collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Profile Picture
  • Email address
  • Unique user identifier (provided by Google or Apple)

We do not access your passwords or any sensitive authentication data.

b. Scanned Gold Data

When you scan a gold using our app, the following data may be collected:

  • Images of golds captured through the app
  • AI-generated analysis or metadata related to the scanned golds

This data is stored securely to allow you to revisit your scan history and access AI analysis results.

c. Interaction Analytics

When you interact with our app, the following data may be collected:

  • User interactions with the app
  • App usage metrics
  • Performance metrics
  • Error/Crash logs

This data is stored securely to allow us to improve the performance and reliability of the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide AI-powered gold analysis
  • Save your scanned golds and associated data
  • Improve the performance and reliability of the app
  • Enable account-related features (like cloud storage of scans)

We do not use your data for advertising or third-party marketing.

3. Third-Party Services

We confirm that any third party with whom our app shares user data provide the same or equal protection of user data as stated in the app’s privacy policy.

4. Your Privacy Choices

You can:

  • Use the app without signing in (with limited functionality)
  • Delete your account and all associated scan data upon request inside app
  • Revoke app access from your Google or Apple account settings

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes via the app or by updating the "Effective Date" above.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide the app’s services.

If you use Altinos without signing in, scanned images and AI analysis are not stored anywhere in our system.

If you sign in with Google or Apple, your account data and scan history are stored securely in our systems until you request deletion.

You may:

  • Request deletion of your account and all associated scan data at any time through the settings
